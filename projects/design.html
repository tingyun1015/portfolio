The Challenge

Data indicates that over 20% of people skip breakfast entirely, whilst approximately 60% regularly consume unhealthy options. Breakfast should ideally be a relaxing start to the day; however, rushed morning routines prevent people from preparing healthy, delicious meals. This forces them to rely on quick convenience foods or skip eating altogether. To address this, we developed two core solutions.

01. Healthy Breakfast Promotion Campaign

Whilst most people understand the principles of healthy eating, few put them into practice, highlighting a significant gap between knowledge and behaviour. Beyond merely reiterating the importance of breakfast, this initiative encourages individuals to evaluate their current morning routines and make sustainable improvements. The true value of breakfast extends beyond dietary choices—it is about cultivating positive lifestyle habits.

02. Healthy Breakfast Delivery Service

  • Target Audience: Individuals aged 20–40 who skip breakfast or compromise on nutrition due to busy schedules.
  • Value Proposition: To empower consumers to achieve a healthy morning routine in the most effortless way possible.
